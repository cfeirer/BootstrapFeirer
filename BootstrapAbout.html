Meet our two favorite dog trainers,Laura London and Nate Schoemer The trainers have the individual meet the dog at the shelter or bring the dog to their house depending on their mobility. Once the individual and the dog have met and are a successful match, training begins. First this starts with bonding, then basic commands. After basic commands are learned, professional dog trainers takes the dog to another training facility for the month to focus and learn special commands needed to complete certain tasks for the inividual.
